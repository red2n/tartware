# Property Types & Classifications

## Lodging Categories

| Category | Characteristics | PMS Requirements |
|----------|-----------------|------------------|
| **Full-Service Hotel** | 100-1000+ rooms, F&B, banquets, spa, concierge | Complex folio routing, group management, POS integration |
| **Limited-Service Hotel** | 50-200 rooms, minimal F&B, self-service | Streamlined check-in, automated housekeeping |
| **Boutique Hotel** | 10-100 rooms, unique design, personalized service | Guest preference tracking, flexible rate structures |
| **Resort** | Destination property, multiple outlets, activities | Package management, activity booking, multi-currency |
| **Vacation Rental** | Individual units, owner management | Owner statements, split revenue, dynamic pricing |
| **Hostel** | Dormitory beds, shared facilities | Bed-level inventory, per-person pricing |
| **Extended Stay** | Weekly/monthly rates, kitchenettes | Long-stay rate plans, utility billing |
| **Casino Hotel** | Gaming, comps, player tracking | Comp management, player rating integration |

## Chain Scales (STR Classification)

```
Luxury         → ADR > $250  (Ritz-Carlton, Four Seasons, St. Regis)
Upper Upscale  → ADR $175-250 (Marriott, Hyatt, Westin)
Upscale        → ADR $125-175 (Hilton Garden Inn, Courtyard)
Upper Midscale → ADR $90-125  (Holiday Inn, Hampton Inn)
Midscale       → ADR $60-90   (Best Western, Wyndham)
Economy        → ADR < $60    (Motel 6, Super 8, Days Inn)
```

## Ownership Models

| Model | Description | PMS Implications |
|-------|-------------|------------------|
| **Owner-Operated** | Single owner manages property | Full control, simpler reporting |
| **Management Contract** | Owner hires management company | Fee tracking, owner reporting |
| **Franchise** | Brand license, independent owner | Brand standards, PIP compliance |
| **REIT-Owned** | Real estate investment trust | Asset reporting, multiple stakeholders |
| **Condo-Hotel** | Individual unit ownership | Owner usage tracking, revenue split |

## Service Level Expectations

### Full-Service

- 24-hour front desk
- Concierge services
- Room service
- On-site restaurant(s)
- Fitness center
- Business center
- Meeting/banquet facilities
- Valet parking
- Bell services

### Limited-Service

- Front desk (may have limited hours)
- Complimentary breakfast
- Fitness room
- Self-parking
- Guest laundry

### Extended Stay

- Full kitchen in-room
- Weekly housekeeping
- On-site laundry
- Grocery shopping service
- Pet-friendly policies
