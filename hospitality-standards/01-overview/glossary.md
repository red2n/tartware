# Glossary

Industry terminology for Property Management Systems.

## A

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **ADR** | Average Daily Rate - Total room revenue divided by rooms sold |
| **AI-RMS** | AI-powered Revenue Management System - Uses machine learning for automated pricing decisions (e.g., IDeaS G3, Duetto GameChanger, Atomize) |
| **Allotment** | Pre-contracted room block for a group or distribution channel |
| **Amenity** | Service or feature provided to guests (WiFi, parking, breakfast) |
| **Ancillary Revenue** | Non-room revenue from F&B, spa, parking, events; can exceed 18% of total hotel income |
| **ARI** | Availability, Rates, Inventory - Data sent to distribution channels |
| **Attrition** | Percentage of group block rooms not picked up by deadline |

## B

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **BAR** | Best Available Rate - Lowest unrestricted publicly bookable rate |
| **Bed Night** | One bed occupied for one night (hostel metric) |
| **Block** | Rooms held for a group or special allocation |
| **Booking Engine** | Online reservation system on property website |
| **Business Date** | The operational day (may differ from calendar date) |

## C

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Channel Manager** | System that distributes inventory to multiple OTAs |
| **City Ledger** | Accounts receivable for guests who have departed |
| **Comp** | Complimentary - Provided free of charge |
| **CRS** | Central Reservation System - Chain-level booking platform |
| **Cutoff Date** | Deadline for group block pickup before release |

## D

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Day Use** | Same-day check-in and check-out (typically 6-8 hours) |
| **Digital Key** | Smartphone-based room access via BLE/NFC; replaces physical key cards |
| **Direct Bill** | Invoice sent to company, payment collected later |
| **Displacement Analysis** | Evaluating whether accepting a group booking displaces higher-value transient demand |
| **DND** | Do Not Disturb - Guest declined housekeeping service |
| **Double Occupancy** | Two guests in one room |

## E-F

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Early Departure** | Guest checking out before scheduled date |
| **EBITDAR** | Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, Amortization, and Rent - Key profitability metric for hotel valuation |
| **ESG** | Environmental, Social, and Governance - Sustainability framework increasingly tied to revenue strategy; 78% of guests prefer eco-friendly stays |
| **Flash Report** | Quick daily metrics summary for management |
| **Flow-through** | Percentage of incremental revenue that reaches the bottom line as profit |
| **Folio** | Guest bill/account showing all charges and payments |
| **Front of House** | Guest-facing operations (front desk, concierge, bell) |

## G

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **GDS** | Global Distribution System - Travel agent booking networks (Sabre, Amadeus) |
| **GOP** | Gross Operating Profit |
| **GOPPAR** | Gross Operating Profit Per Available Room - Emerging as the primary profitability metric (preferred over RevPAR by 33.7% of revenue leaders per Cornell/Kimes study) |
| **Group** | 10+ rooms with shared billing or purpose |
| **Guest Folio** | Individual guest's charges during stay |

## H-I

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **HAPI** | Hospitality API - OpenAPI 3.0-based REST standard replacing legacy HTNG XML/SOAP |
| **House Count** | Total persons in-house (includes additional occupants) |
| **House Use** | Rooms used internally (staff housing, maintenance) |
| **HTNG** | Hotel Technology Next Generation - Industry integration standards body |
| **Incidentals** | Charges beyond room rate (minibar, phone, laundry) |
| **In-House** | Currently checked-in guest |

## L-M

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Late Checkout** | Departure after standard checkout time (fee may apply) |
| **LOS** | Length of Stay - Number of nights |
| **Master Folio** | Main billing account for a group |
| **MLOS** | Minimum Length of Stay restriction |
| **Market Segment** | Guest classification (transient, corporate, group, etc.) |

## N-O

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Net ADR** | ADR after subtracting distribution commissions and transaction costs |
| **Night Audit** | End-of-day process to close business date and run reports |
| **No-Show** | Guest with guaranteed reservation who doesn't arrive |
| **NRevPAR** | Net Revenue Per Available Room - RevPAR minus acquisition costs (commissions, transaction fees) |
| **Occupancy** | Percentage of available rooms sold |
| **OOO** | Out of Order - Room unavailable due to maintenance |
| **OOS** | Out of Service - Room temporarily unavailable |
| **Open Pricing** | Pricing strategy that allows independent rate adjustments per segment, channel, and room type (vs. fixed BAR modifiers). Pioneered by Duetto; 5% customer retention increase → 20-90% profit growth |
| **OTA** | Online Travel Agency (Booking.com, Expedia, Hotels.com) |
| **Overbooking** | Accepting more reservations than available rooms |

## P

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **PAR** | Per Available Room (as in RevPAR) |
| **PIP** | Property Improvement Plan - Brand-mandated upgrades |
| **PMS** | Property Management System |
| **POS** | Point of Sale - System for restaurant, bar, retail |
| **Pickup** | New reservations on the books for future dates |

## R

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Rack Rate** | Published maximum/list rate |
| **Rate Parity** | Same rate across all distribution channels (narrow parity now standard in EU after anti-competitive rulings) |
| **RevPAC** | Revenue Per Available Customer - Total revenue divided by available guests; measures per-guest value |
| **RevPAR** | Revenue Per Available Room - ADR × Occupancy |
| **RGI** | Revenue Generation Index - Property RevPAR ÷ Compset RevPAR × 100 (STR/CoStar benchmark) |
| **RMS** | Revenue Management System - Software for automated pricing/inventory optimization (IDeaS G3, Duetto, Atomize) |
| **Rooming List** | Group guest names matched to specific rooms |
| **RP-OS** | Revenue & Profit Operating System - Duetto's integrated platform combining revenue and cost signals |
| **Run of House** | Room type assigned at check-in, not guaranteed |

## S

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Shoulder Date** | Days immediately before/after peak event dates |
| **Single Occupancy** | One guest in one room |
| **Stayover** | Guest remaining for another night |
| **STR** | Now CoStar with STR Benchmark - Industry benchmarking with 94,000 hotels and 12M rooms in sample |

## T

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **TRevPAR** | Total Revenue Per Available Room (includes ancillary) - Ranked top-2 most important benchmark by revenue leaders |
| **TRevPOR** | Total Revenue Per Occupied Room - Revenue per occupied room across all departments |
| **Turn-down** | Evening room service (bed prep, amenities refresh) |
| **Transient** | Individual (non-group) guest |
| **Total Revenue Management** | Holistic approach optimizing all revenue streams (rooms, F&B, spa, events, parking) rather than rooms alone |

## U-V

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Upsell** | Offering higher room category or add-on services |
| **Virtual Card** | OTA-generated payment card for property collection |

## W-Y

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Walk** | Relocating a guest to another hotel due to oversell |
| **Wash** | Rate adjustment to compensate for service failure |
| **Yield Management** | Dynamic pricing based on demand forecasting (narrower than revenue management; tactical vs. strategic) |
