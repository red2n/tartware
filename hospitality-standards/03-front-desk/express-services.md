# Express Services

## Express Check-in

### Pre-arrival Registration

Guest completes registration before arrival:

| Element | Collection Method |
|---------|-------------------|
| ID verification | Upload photo or scan on arrival |
| Payment | Card authorization pre-arrival |
| Preferences | Digital form or app |
| Signature | Digital signature |
| Arrival time | ETA notification |

### Key Ready Programs

| Method | Process |
|--------|---------|
| **Key packet** | Pre-encoded keys in envelope at desk |
| **Key kiosk** | Self-service key encoding station |
| **Mobile key** | Bluetooth/NFC via smartphone app |
| **Smart lock** | Direct lock code communication |

## Express Check-out

### Folio Delivery Options

| Method | Timing | Guest Action |
|--------|--------|--------------|
| Email folio | Immediately at checkout | Review at convenience |
| Under door | Night before departure | Review overnight |
| Mobile app | Real-time during stay | Approve charges |
| Kiosk printout | At departure | Print on demand |

### Zero-balance Checkout

When guest folio is $0.00 or pre-settled:
- System auto-generates folio
- Keys automatically deactivate at checkout time
- Room status updates to dirty
- Thank you email sent

## Mobile Check-in/out

### Requirements

| Component | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| App | Native iOS/Android |
| Digital key | BLE 4.0 or NFC capable locks |
| ID verification | Photo upload or ID scan |
| Payment | Stored card or mobile wallet |
| Notifications | Push for room ready |

### Mobile Key Technology

| Technology | Range | Battery Impact | Industry Status (2026) |
|------------|-------|----------------|------------------------|
| **Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE)** | 1-10 meters | Low | Dominant standard |
| **NFC** | < 10 cm | Minimal | Growing (Apple Wallet integration) |
| **WiFi** | Room proximity | Moderate | Limited adoption |
| **Ultra-Wideband (UWB)** | Precise (<30cm) | Low | Emerging for premium properties |
